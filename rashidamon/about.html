ABOUT US

We enable the young to become responsible decision makers of tomorrow. We take these young powerhouses to create a movement for a cleaner and greener tomorrow, a movement that reverberates throughout the society, a movement which compels policy makers to create sustainable cities.

INTRODUCTION

CLEAN-India is an acronym which stands for Community Led Environment Action Network, a platform for youth..

OUTREACH

Today, the programme has moved much beyond the boundaries of Delhi where it took roots. In collaboration with NGOs, the programme has taken roots in 78 other towns and cities extending from Ranikhet in the North to Nagercoil in the South.
