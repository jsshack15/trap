Welcome To Rashida Monir Charitable Trust

Rashida Monir Charitable Trust is a voluntary, non-profit organization established on 12th April, 2013 for the upliftment of education in India and eradication of poverty by making poor people self reliable. Combining consultancy, training and skilled volunteers, our Trust allows people to look beyond their day to day resource constraints and maximise their impacts in the educational sector. We believe in "It is better to light the candle than to curse the darkness"- Chinese Proverb
